The Vision

Where Nature Meets Luxury

Hidden Lakes is an exclusive 80-acre residential development offering a rare opportunity to build your dream home on estate-sized lots with breathtaking lake views in Iowa City's most sought-after school district.

Estate-Sized Lots

Generous 0.5 to 1.5 acre homesites offering privacy, space, and room for your custom dream home with all the amenities you desire.

Two Scenic Lakes

Premium waterfront living with two beautiful lakes creating a serene backdrop for your family's new chapter. Select lots offer direct water views.

All Builders Welcome

Choose your preferred builder and architect to create a truly custom home. No builder restrictions means your vision, your way.

West High District

Located in Iowa City's prestigious West High School district, known for academic excellence and comprehensive extracurricular programs.

City Water Access

Enjoy the benefits of a county subdivision with the convenience of city water service. Modern infrastructure meets natural beauty.

Premium Location

Just 15 minutes to downtown Iowa City and Coral Ridge Mall, with downtown Cedar Rapids only 28 minutes away. The perfect balance of seclusion and accessibility.
